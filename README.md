# The Olympus Engine

The Olympus Engine is a rust 3d engine focused on simplicity using the [MIT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MIT_License) license.

It abstract engine concept with deities names.


# Deities

## Apollo

The national divinity of the Greeks, Apollo has been recognized as a god of archery, music and dance, truth and prophecy, healing and diseases, the Sun and light, poetry, and more.

Apollo is used as abstraction for Audio and music.

ref : [Apollo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollo)

## Atlas

In Greek mythology, Atlas (/ˈætləs/; Greek: Ἄτλας, Átlas) is a Titan condemned to hold up the heavens or sky for eternity. It is also a collection of maps.

Atlas is used to create stages and query actors whereabout.

ref : [Atlas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlas_(mythology))

## Hercules

Hercules (/ˈhɜːrkjʊˌliːz/, US: /-kjə-/) is the Roman equivalent of the Greek divine hero Heracles, son of Jupiter and the mortal Alcmene. In classical mythology, Hercules is famous for his strength and for his numerous far-ranging adventures including his 12 labours.

Hercules is an expert of labours and tasks. He is used as a taskpool to perform multi-threaded tasks and jobs.

ref : [Hercules](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hercules)

## Iris

In Greek mythology, Iris is a daughter of the gods Thaumas and Electra, the personification and goddess of the rainbow and messenger of the gods.

She is the abstraction for internet connection and multiplayers.

ref : [Iris](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iris_(mythology))

## Kleio

In Greek mythology, Clio, also spelled Kleio is the muse of history. 

She is the abstraction of all files I/O and events (history).

ref : [Kleio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clio)

## Vulcan

Vulcan is the god of fire including the fire of volcanoes, deserts, metalworking and the forge in ancient Roman religion and myth.

He is the abstraction for video rendering.

ref : [Vulcan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vulcan_(mythology))
